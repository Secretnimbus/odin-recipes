Sausage Casserole

Description

A wonderfully warming and filling dish served best with rice or rustic bread.

Ingredients

  • 12 Sausages
  • 150g Chopped Onions
  • 150g Chopped Peppers
  • 400g Tinned Chopped Tomatoes
  • 300ml Chicken Stock
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Pure√©
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Muscavado Sugar
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 Tsp Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Dried Mixed Herbs
  • 400g Tin of Canellini/Haricot Beans
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the sausages in the oven till they brown.
  2. Fry off the peppers and onions in a pan till they brown.
  3. To the peppers and onions add the chopped tomoatoes, beans and stock and leave to reduce.
  4. Once the suasages have finished cooking, chop into small pieces and add to the pan.
  5. Once desired consistency has been reached add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
  6. Serve with rice or bread.
