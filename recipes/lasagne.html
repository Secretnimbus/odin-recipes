Lasagne

Description

A classic, layered pasta dish made with lasagne pasta sheets, a tomoato/meat sauce, a white/béchamel sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 400g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomatoe Pureé
  • 200g Chopped Onions
  • 200g Chopped Peppers
  • 2 Tbsp Italian herbs seasoning
  • 2 Tsp Paprika
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste

For the white sauce

  • 500ml milk
  • 2 Tbsp Butter/Spread
  • 3 Tbsp Plain Flour

Other

  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

Cook the meat sauce

  1. Fry off the peppers and onions till browned.
  2. Add the mince and fry till browned.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and pureé and stir to combine.
  4. Add the herbs and spices and flavour with the salt and pepper.

Cook the white sauce

  1. Add the milk and butter to a pan then heat.
  2. Once the butter has melted sift in the flour and mixed vigourously with a whisk to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep stirring on the heat until the mixture thickens.

Construction

  1. Add three lasagne sheets to a casserole dish and then add a layer of meat sauce followed by a layer of white sauce.
  2. Repeat this two more times and top with the mozzarella cheese.
  3. Put the dish in the over and cook at 200°C for 25-30 mins till the cheese has browned and the pasta sheets are soft.
